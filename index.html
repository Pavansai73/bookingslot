NOTE: Field for cricket matches..

Cricket grounds are the playing fields where the sport of cricket is played. They are typically large, grassy areas with a flat surface for the pitch in the center. Surrounding the pitch is the playing area where fielders position themselves to try and dismiss the batsmen. Cricket grounds can vary in size and shape, with some being circular while others are more oval-shaped. They often have seating areas for spectators to watch the game. Additionally, cricket grounds may have facilities such as pavilions, dressing rooms, and scoreboards to support the players and officials during matches.
